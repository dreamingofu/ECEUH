// Key Formulas

02 — QUICK_REFERENCE
Impedance
Z = R + jX
Complex impedance — resistance R (real) and reactance X (imaginary). ZL = jωL, ZC = 1/jωC
Resonant Frequency
ω₀ = 1/√(LC)
Natural resonant frequency of an LC or RLC circuit in rad/s. f₀ = ω₀ / 2π in Hz.
Average Power
P = ½|V||I|cosθ
Average (real) power in watts. θ is the angle between voltage and current phasors.
Quality Factor
Q = ω₀L / R
Ratio of energy stored to energy dissipated per cycle. Higher Q = sharper resonance peak.
Apparent Power
S = P + jQ
Complex power S (VA). Real part P (watts), imaginary part Q (VAR). |S| = Vrms × Irms.
Transfer Function
H(jω) = Vout/Vin
Ratio of output to input phasors. Magnitude gives gain, angle gives phase shift vs. frequency.
